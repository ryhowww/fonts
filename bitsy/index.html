What changed

Angled y descender

The right diagonal continues as the descender instead of curving left. Inspired by Questrial's clean geometric approach.

yet yes yell
yet yes yell

Rectangular dots

Circular dots on i, j, period, colon, semicolon, and punctuation replaced with rectangles. Taller at light weights, stumpier at heavy.

i j . : ; ! ?
i j . : ; ! ?

Raised t

The t ascender raised to close the gap with the i dot, bringing vertical rhythm to mixed lowercase text.

tilt tight
tilt tight

Aligned punctuation

Period and colon bottom sit on the baseline. Semicolon top matches colon. Everything lines up.
